SeaOS

SeaOS is an interactive prototype that centralizes updates on maritime conditions, sustainable fishing, and safety. It simplifies access to weather, sea status, and fisheries information, and includes a fisherman section for catch records and local activity, connecting people with the ocean.​ SeaOS is especially relevant for Madeira, a Portuguese island known for its unique connection to the sea.

Aura

An innovative virtual assistant designed to revolutionize the tourism experience by providing personalized itineraries, sustainable suggestions, and seamless smart home integration. The system acts as a proactive assistant embedded in an interactive monitor, offering contextualized recommendations based on tourist preferences, weather conditions, local events, and dining options.

EchoSense

An app designed to facilitates the recapture of equipment deployed underwater. This project addresses critical challenges faced by marine researchers in locating and retrieving underwater instruments, particularly when they lack active propulsion and rely on dead reckoning for positioning.

Figma Canva ProCreate

WaveRewards

It is a gamified web platform designed for sustainable canoeing. With weekly challenges and a simple experience, the goal is to promote people to practice physical activity and simultaneously protect the sea, in line with Atlantic Innoblue Communities.
